About

Cepheus is a fintech company established by a group of enthusiasts with substantial experience in online banking, asset management, law, and international trade. We aspire to become a global payment network by building a portfolio of payment institution licenses in many countries and uniting them under one digital umbrella. Cepheus focuses on introducing comprehensive financial services to the community of individuals and small and medium-sized enterprises.

Cepheus

Strategy

We intend to provide fast and efficient financial services through the latest fintech innovations because we want to make the world a better place and change people's lives through our solutions.

Cepheus sees its future in establishing long-term relationships with our customers based on trust, loyalty, and reciprocity. We set a high bar for excellence to ensure diversity and inclusivity are always at the heart of our company mindset.
